Our Team

Meet Our Global Chess Enthusiasts!

pAt the heart of our Colombian Chess Haven is a diverse and passionate team that spans the corners of the globe. What started as a collaboration between two best friends who shared a love for chess has blossomed into a worldwide community of chess enthusiasts, each bringing their unique perspectives, skills, and talents to the board.



Nicolas Escobar and Daniel Tovar - Founders
United by a deep-rooted passion for chess and a lifelong friendship, we embarked on this journey to create a platform that celebrates our shared love for the game. Hailing from Colombia, our dream was to establish a hub that brings together chess players from all walks of life. With determination and creativity, we laid the foundation for this global chess haven.

Jackson Cast - Strategy Guru (New York, USA)
An analytical mind and a penchant for strategic thinking, Jackson is our resident chess strategist. Hailing from the bustling city of New York, their insights and tactical acumen are a driving force behind the intellectual richness of our platform.

Camilo Morales - Community Champion (Bogotá, Colombia)
Representing our Colombian roots, Camilo infuses our community with warmth and camaraderie. With a deep connection to local chess clubs and events, they ensure that our Colombian Chess Haven remains grounded in the vibrant chess culture of Bogotá.

David Hans - Technical Wizard (Berlin, Germany)
From the tech-savvy streets of Berlin, David casts a spell of innovation over our website. Their coding prowess and technical expertise keep our virtual chessboard running smoothly, providing users with an engaging and seamless experience.

Our team is a mosaic of cultures, talents, and experiences, united by the love of chess and a shared vision for our Colombian Chess Haven. From the bustling streets of New York to the serene landscapes of Sydney, we believe that chess transcends borders, language, and time zones. Together, we're not just players; we're a global community that celebrates the beauty of the game and the friendships it forges.



Join us as we make our moves on the global chessboard, one strategic step at a time.
