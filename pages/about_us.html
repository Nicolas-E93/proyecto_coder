About us

At the heart of this digital space beats the passion for chess that brought two best friends together

Born out of our shared enthusiasm for the game, we proudly present to you a chess website that captures the essence of our Colombian chess community.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to provide a haven for chess enthusiasts across Colombia. As devoted fans ourselves, we recognized the need for a platform that not only celebrates the beauty and strategy of chess but also fosters a sense of unity among players of all levels. With the collective expertise of our dedicated team, we've crafted a virtual home where chess lovers can come together to learn, compete, and revel in the intricate dance of the pieces.

What sets us apart is our commitment to the Colombian chess spirit. We've sprinkled the site with a touch of our cultural vibrancy, incorporating elements that pay homage to the rich heritage of our beloved country. From the iconic landmarks that grace our design to the warmth of our community interactions, you'll find a reflection of Colombia's heartwarming hospitality in every corner of our website.

Whether you're a grandmaster in the making or just starting your chess journey, our website is designed with you in mind. Engage in thrilling matches with fellow Colombians, explore insightful articles on chess strategy, or dive into the captivating history of the game. Every move you make here brings you closer to the heart of Colombian chess.

But this site is more than just a virtual board; it's a testament to friendship and shared dreams. Our journey from avid chess fans to creators of this platform has been fueled by the belief that chess has the power to bring people together, no matter their background or skill level. As best friends, we've poured our hearts into every pixel, every line of code, and every strategic concept to create a space that embodies the joy we've experienced through the game.


So, welcome once again to our Colombian Chess Haven! Join us in celebrating the magic of chess, the beauty of our culture, and the bonds of friendship that have made this website a reality. Together, let's continue to shape the chess landscape in Colombia and beyond.


Checkmate and camaraderie,
Nicolas and Daniel
Co-Founders of the Colombian Chess Haven
